URL Spoofing Trick 3 - Random/Unrelated/Trustworthy Domain, with Brand in Subdomain

A Phisher can include the brand name into the subdomain of a URL in combination with a random/unrelated/trustworthy domain name.
Example:- http://paypal. mark-chippy.com/account-setup/ for Paypal or http://www.amazon.account.com/ for Amazon. Here the subdomain contains the brand name in combination with a random/unrelated/trustworthy domain name.
