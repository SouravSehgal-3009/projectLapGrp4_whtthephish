URL Spoofing Trick 6 - Introducing Typos

Phishers can register domains which resemble the targeted domain, but have a typo. One special case of the typo is swapping letters in the original domain name.

Example:- The Phisher can register micosoft.com to impersonte microsoft.com or mircosoft.com to impersonate microsoft.com.
